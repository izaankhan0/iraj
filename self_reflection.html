Journal Prompts for self-reflection

Take a moment to reflect on your feelings and find clarity through journaling.

1:

how am I feeling right now?

2:

what can I do to feel better?

3:

Am I where I want to be mentally and physically?

4:

What or who is stopping me from making this change?

5:

what makes me truly happy?

6:

what would the best version of myself do?

7:

what is my definition of success?
