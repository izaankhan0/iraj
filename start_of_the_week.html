Journal Prompts to start your week

Take a moment to reflect on your feelings and find clarity through journaling.

1:

how am I feeling right now?

2:

how can I make this week better than my last?

3:

What do I want to remember from last week ?

4:

what do I need to let go of from last week?

5:

What are my intentions for this week?

6:

3 goals for this week, small or big.

7:

3 things I will do for myself and others this week.
