Journal Prompts for self-love

Take a moment to reflect on your feelings and find clarity through journaling.

1:

what do I love about myself?

2:

what am I proud of?

3:

what is my most recent accomplishment, it does not have to be big?

4:

where do I feel most at home, why?

5:

a list of 5 things that make my heart sing!?

6:

what am I really good at?

7:

a time where I felt confident in myself, who was I with?
