Journal Prompts for When You Feel overwhelmed

Take a moment to reflect on your feelings and find clarity through journaling.

1:

Do you know why you are overwhelmed?

2:

write down everything on your mind right now.

3:

what self care activities can you do to feel calm?

4:

imagine a perfect place for relaxation. Write about it in detail.

5:

how can I re-prioritize my to do list?

6:

what is one small step you can take to make today easier?

7:

name a few things you need to let go of.
