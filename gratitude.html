Journal Prompts for gratitude

Take a moment to reflect on your feelings and find clarity through journaling.

1:

5 things I am grateful for today?

2:

how did you take care of yourself today, or plan of taking care of yourself?

3:

what did you do this past week that made you happy?

4:

what is something that worked out better than expected?

5:

what can you do this week to make it a bit better?
